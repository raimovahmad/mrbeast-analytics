Analyst Insights
🚀
Shorts Dominance
Shorts average 340M views per video — 40% more than Long-form (244M). Shorter format wins on discovery and rewatch rate.
💡
Engagement Paradox
Long videos drive 4.5x more comments than Shorts (110K vs 25K avg). Audiences discuss — but don't like — long content as much.
📈
Peak: Oct 2023
October 2023 was the strongest month: 490M avg views per video across 3 uploads. Highest single-month performance in the dataset.
💰
Best Like Rate
"I Tipped A Pizza Driver A Car" hit 3.89% like rate — the highest in the dataset. Emotional, generous content drives maximum approval.
🎯
Recent Slowdown
2025–2026 uploads average only 80M views — far below the channel average. New videos need time to accumulate; this is a recency lag effect.
🏅
Golden Formula
Top performing videos share 3 traits: relatable premise + huge prize + Shorts format. "World's Fastest Cleaners" (717M views) is the perfect example.
